What is MusicalPT?

Physical therapy (PT) is a crucial and highly beneficial activity that supports recovery from acute injury and generally promotes mobility and functioning throughout the lifespan. However, adherence to at-home PT is a major challenge given boredom, motivation, and difficulties tracking progress.

Our project leverages the therapeutic power of music to provide intuitive, real-time feedback and adaptive guidance during exercises using state-of-the-art computer vision and music generation algorithms.

Who We Are

We are Empower Lab at Dartmouth College, designing interactive technologies to empower people and promote social good. Our focus is on health equity, behavior change technologies, and accessible education tools.
